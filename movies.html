Top 7 movies about journalism

There are many movies about famous articles or journalism in general but some stand out more than others. Some reference serious articles and events and the important impact of journalism while others capture the magic and fun that comes with it.

Citizen Kane

Directed by Orson Welles

A classic depicting a reporter’s investigation into the meaning of Charles Kane’s last words.

The Secret Life of Walter Mitty

Directed by Ben Stiller

A film that captures the magic in adventure and the importance of every journalist’s work in their news organization.

She Said

Directed by Maria Schrader

This film tells the story of when New York Times journalists investigated the allegations against Harvey Weinstein which led to the #MeToo movement.

Almost Famous

Directed by Cameron Crowe

There are many different subjects you can focus on in journalism and one of those is music. This film dives into the story of a boy given the chance to write an article for Rolling Stone which leads to him following a band and growing close to them.

Spotlight

Directed by Tom McCarthy

A film telling the story of an investigative story into the sexual abuse in the Catholic Church.

The Post

Directed by Steven Spielberg

A film showing how reporters and editors at the Washington Post reported on the Pentagon Papers.

All The President's Men

Directed by Alan J. Pakula

This film explores the investigative story of the Watergate scandal by the Washington Post.
